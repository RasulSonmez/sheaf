Frequently asked questions

Are my documents private?

Yes. Everything Sheaf does — scanning, OCR, signing, locking, merging, and more — happens entirely on your device. Your files and their contents are never uploaded to us. See our Privacy Policy.

What do I get with Sheaf Pro?

Pro unlocks the power tools — Sign, Lock, Split & Extract, and Annotate — and removes the free limits on text recognition, compression, and merging. It’s available as a yearly subscription (with a free trial) or a one-time lifetime purchase.

How do I start the free trial?

Open the app, tap any Pro feature or the upgrade screen, and choose the yearly plan. The free trial starts automatically and converts to a paid subscription only if you don’t cancel before it ends.

How do I restore a purchase on a new device?

Open the upgrade screen and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you’re signed in to the same Apple Account you used to buy Sheaf.

How do I cancel or manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone or iPad, open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions, select Sheaf, and cancel or change your plan there.

I forgot the password on a locked PDF.

For your security, the password is never stored and never leaves your device, so we can’t recover it. You’ll need the original password to unlock the file.
